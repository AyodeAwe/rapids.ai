Hardware

RAPIDS is used with NVIDIA GPU hardware. There is a wide range of compatible hardware, from commercial gaming cards, enterprise level multi-gpu systems, and cloud based resources. Find out more on NVIDA's product page and our compatibility list.

Software

RAPIDS is comprised of a growing list of libraries and APIs to use for your specific area. Based on Apache arrow, they all work well together while providing GPU accelerated performance. See details below.


[ Graphic on how all these sections fit ]

Developers

Part of a wide open source and growing community, several projects have built off of RAPIDS or integrated with RAPIDS to take advantage of its ease and performance. See more below.
